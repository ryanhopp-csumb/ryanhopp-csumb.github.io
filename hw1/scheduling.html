Process Scheduling


The 4 different scheduling policies:

  1. FIFO (First In, First Out) - Runs the job that arrived first, first.
  2. SJF (Shortest Job First) - Runs the shortest job first.
  3. STCF (Shortest Time to Completion First) - Runs the job with the shortest amount of time left.
  4. Round Robin - Runs every job for a short amount of time, then switches to a different job.
